To the love of my life,

Happy birthday! I hope you have a great day today. I’m going to preface this with a warning. I’m not very good at writing birthday messages but I really wanted to give you one so here goes. I’ve known you for almost 9 months now but it already feels so natural to have you around. Even months ago, I would get bored and immediately think “let’s text Max”. I always looked forward to your random summons (even though I made fun of you for it- touch grass Maxwell). It was almost like your presence reassured me in some way. I’m normally always scanning for ulterior motives but with you it was almost like I could take a break and just chill out.

I’m going to be so for reals. It never mattered to me whether we were actually platonic or romantic (obviously, I had a preference for one maybe probably). In my head, I just knew you were someone I wanted to keep in my life. I drag platonic!era Max a lot but, in all honesty, I didn’t actually mind it most of the time. Yes- there were times that I was annoyed and confused (I hate not knowing whats going on) but mostly I enjoyed every minute with you. You said you were worried that I only liked you because you were nice or how you made me feel safe but that’s not it at all.

The first thing I noticed was your intellect. You may doubt yourself at times but it’s obvious to everyone with a brain cell that you possess one of the brightest minds. Its in the ways you break down issues and relay them to others. I saw it in the ways you interacted with my words, the little look on your face when you are analysis mode and the little proud chuckle u have when you realise you’ve figured out the dynamic of the room. I’m in your head (I know the gist of what you are thinking at all times). I don’t agree with every conclusion you come to but even back then I couldn’t help but view you as so beautifully intelligent.

Then, I learnt about how genuinely you face every sunrise and sunset. It might not be a big deal to you but I admire it so much. You face every one of your flaws head on and process your feelings even if they inconvenience you. When I pointed out you were being avoidant, you thought about it for a moment and even if you didn’t initially agree with it, you took it onboard and worked to better yourself. Most people can never admit they have a problem- let alone try to fix it. It’s something I still struggle with (refer to my denial of basic facts). But, you have the ability to look at your flaws with such radical acceptance. You might not think it’s a strength but it opens up so many avenues for change and progress. Remember, acceptance is the first step. Since you’ve started your ascension, I’ve always seen you as the most stunning work in progress. You may not see it but every dip and fall is still a step forward and you are changing everyday. I makes me want to support you and push you forward so you can reach the goals you set for yourself. That said, make no mistake- that is not to say I fell in love with your potential (that would be dumb). I fell in love with your perspective in life, you ideals, your morals, your consideration towards everyone (even people you don’t like), the fact that even though you love me you still call me out when I’m being questionable… The list goes on and on.

I fell in love with you as a person. There are so many things about you that I can’t help but grow immeasurably fond of. And, I say this without rose-tinted glasses because I have always thought that you are one of the most beautiful souls I have ever had the honour to befriend and love. The fact you are also completely enamoured by me is just the cherry on top.

I said this before we got together and I’ll say it again. I’m so excited to see who you’ll become and I will do anything (well, within reason and realistically) I can to help you ascend into who you want to be.

I love you so much and I hope I get to celebrate many more birthdays with you.
